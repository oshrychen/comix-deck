Comix Deck — Support

Live show control for iPad: soundtrack, background music, and lighting.

Getting started

On launch, choose a mode:

iPad only — everything runs locally on your iPad.
iPad + Maestro — connect to a Maestro music server on your local network.

The three modules

  • Show Player — load your show’s audio and run it live with a large GO / STOP transport, cues, a performance stopwatch, and fades.
  • Apple Music — play background music from your Apple Music library before the show, with shuffle and crossfade.
  • Lighting Console — a DMX console over Art‑Net: per‑channel faders, fixtures, and instant scene presets.

Hardware notes

  • The Lighting Console sends Art‑Net/DMX to a lighting node on your local network. Set the node’s IP in Settings.
  • Network mode requires a Maestro server on your network.
  • The Show Player and Apple Music work on their own, no extra hardware needed.
